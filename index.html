Top 3 cities to live in India in 2019

Gurgaon

Gurgaon is a city located in the North Indian state of Haryana. It’s a leading industrial and financial hub with the third-highest per capita income in India. It has multiple IT firms and is a hub for business process outsourcing and home to Indian branch offices to Fortune 500 companies. Moreover, this city is also has a high demand in the real estate sector and contributes major to the city’s economy.The main advantage of moving to Gurgaon is abundant job opportunities with a good salary package.

Bangalore

Bangalore is the capital Indian state of Karnataka which is one of the growing metropolitan cities in India. It is called the “Silicon Valley of India” and the IT hub of India with growing opportunities in the IT sector. This city is the home for many startups and well-established companies in various sectors which results in a lot of job opportunities for freshers. Specifically, in the IT domain, you can find all renowned multinational companies, tech parks over here, which has created more job opportunities for job seekers.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital city of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. Well known as the “City of Nizams” is one of the fourth most populous cities in India. According to a survey by mercer, Hyderabad is the most livable city in India for Expats. After Bangalore, Hyderabad is one of the growing and emerging cities in the IT sector. Hyderabad has the renowned companies like BHEL, DRDO, CCMB, SEBI, etc. The growth of IT, financial and insurance services has boosted the rapid growth in the service industry.
